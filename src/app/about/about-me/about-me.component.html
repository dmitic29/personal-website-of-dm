Brief History

I was born in Belgrade, Serbia where I grew up and spent the first 19 years of my life. Growing up I participated in many extracurricular activities including playing the violin, rowing crew, track and field, learining French, and more.
As a high school student I spent lots of time building and fixing computers in my godfather's shop. I also dedicated quite some time to my hobby - making music. You can listen to one of the songs I made in the embedded SoundCloud window at the bottom of the page.

College

In 2015, I started my bachelor's degree at Webster University in the Netherlands (satellite campus). Initially, my degree was Business Administration, but I quickly realized that I would prefer to pursue a more challenging academic path.
In my sophomore year I moved to Webster University's main campus in Saint Louis, MO in the United States and changed my major to Finance with a minor in Computer Science. It turned out that Computer Science was far more interesting than I initially expected, so in 2018 I converted my minor in Computer Science into a second major. After moving to Saint Louis, MO, I also became a member of the varsity track and field team, and a student-worker for the Housing Department of the university.
